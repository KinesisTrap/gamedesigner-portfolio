Playtest ReadyNoise & PressureSkill investment

Core loop: each lock is a set of colored D6 pins with a revealed target. Matching fixes a pin; unresolved dice re-roll with Modifier Dice from skill.

Game Mechanics

Social Encounter

Diplomacy deck · design draft

Personality-driven defenses, Influence, and Tension tracks.

Design DraftInfluence / TensionPersonality-driven
The NPC presents social defenses based on personality. Players respond from their Diplomacy Deck before Tension hits the limit.

Character Profiles from three trait pairs assemble a 15-card Defense Deck. Players use six Diplomacy Types — Seal, Blade, Heart, Mask, Coin, Freedom.

TrackRangeEffect
Influence-2 to 10Progress toward the goal
Tension0 to 10Limit fails the encounter

Personality drives defense style — Lawful NPCs escalate Tension formally; Outlaw NPCs disrupt structure and reduce Influence.
