Lockpicking System

Playtest Ready · v0.1

Access to locked doors, chests, safes and archives during exploration and heist scenarios. Creates infiltration tension, rewards Lockpicking skill investment, and never hard-locks progress on failed rolls.

Core loop: each lock is a set of colored D6 “pins” with a revealed target combination. Matching a die to its target fixes that pin; unresolved dice are re-rolled with Modifier Dice from skill level.

DifficultyPinsRecommended Noise Threshold
Easy32–3
Medium42
Hard51–2

Failed attempts accumulate local Lock Pressure; past the Noise Threshold it becomes global Scene Noise — stealth consequences without blocking progress. Balanced with Monte Carlo simulations.

Social Encounter System

Design Draft
The NPC presents a sequence of social defenses based on their personality. A player responds with cards from their Diplomacy Deck. The group tries to gain enough Influence before the conversation reaches its Tension limit.

Each NPC has a Character Profile from three trait pairs (Legality, Temperament, Generosity), assembling a 15-card Diplomacy Defense Deck. Players respond with cards from six Diplomacy Types — Seal, Blade, Heart, Mask, Coin, Freedom.

Round structure: gain Tension → draw 3 cards → reveal 1 NPC Defense Card → play response cards → resolve Influence/Tension → discard & repeat.

TrackRangeEffect
Influence-2 to 10Progress toward the conversation goal
Tension0 to 10Reaching the NPC’s limit fails the encounter

Design intent: personality drives defense style — Lawful NPCs escalate Tension formally; Outlaw NPCs disrupt structure and reduce Influence.

Design pillars

Playable Vertical Slice
  • •  Two resources — per-turn Arcane (resets) vs. bankable Essence — tempo vs. investment.
  • •  Two identities — Fire (aggression and reach) vs. Water (sustain and resilience).
  • •  Free play, paid acquisition — cards in hand are free; Arcane and Essence buy new cards into the deck.
  • •  Automatic, deterministic combat — randomness limited to deck shuffles and market order.
